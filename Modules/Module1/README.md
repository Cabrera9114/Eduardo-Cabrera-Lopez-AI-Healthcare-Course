# Module 1: Medical Terminology Identification

## 📌 Overview
This module focused on understanding key medical terminology used in healthcare and how these terms relate to artificial intelligence applications in the medical field.

## 🧠 What I Learned
In this assignment, I learned the importance of medical terminology in ensuring clear communication within healthcare systems. I also explored how AI technologies rely on standardized medical language to analyze data, support decision-making, and improve patient outcomes.

## ⚙️ Work Completed
For this assignment, I identified and analyzed several important healthcare and AI-related terms, including:

- Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- Electronic Health Records (EHRs)
- Machine Learning
- Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS)
- Predictive Analytics
- Telemedicine
- Precision Medicine
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Diagnostic Imaging
- Healthcare Automation

I provided definitions, real-world context, and explained the importance of each term in modern healthcare systems.

## 📊 Key Insights
This assignment helped me understand how AI integrates with healthcare through data-driven systems such as EHRs and predictive models. I also learned how technologies like NLP and diagnostic imaging improve efficiency and accuracy in medical environments.

## ⚠️ Challenges
One challenge I faced was connecting technical AI concepts with healthcare applications. It required a deeper understanding of both fields and how they intersect.

## ✅ Conclusion
This module provided a strong foundation in medical terminology and its connection to AI in healthcare. It showed how important it is for AI professionals to understand healthcare language when building intelligent systems.
